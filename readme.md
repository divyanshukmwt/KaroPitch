# Karo Pitch – Demo Landing Page  
**Intern Assignment Submission**  
Organization: **KaroStartup**

---

# Overview
This project is a **demo landing page website for Karo Pitch**, created as part of the intern assignment provided by **KaroStartup**.

Karo Pitch is envisioned as a platform where **early-stage founders across India — especially from Tier-2, Tier-3 cities and Bharat — can pitch their startups directly to investors**.

The goal of this demo homepage is to clearly communicate:

- the purpose of Karo Pitch  
- how the platform works  
- who it is for  
- how founders can apply  

The design focuses on a **modern startup ecosystem feel**, with a clean interface and clear call-to-actions encouraging founders to participate.

---

# About KaroStartup
Over the past **5+ years**, KaroStartup has built one of India’s largest **startup storytelling platforms**, sharing thousands of founder journeys and inspiring entrepreneurs across the country.

The platform highlights innovative startups, founders, and business ideas across different industries and regions in India.

Karo Pitch is a new initiative by KaroStartup aimed at **bridging the gap between founders and investors** by providing a structured pitching platform.

---

# Project Objective
The objective of this assignment was to design a **modern landing page homepage** that introduces the Karo Pitch platform and encourages founders to apply.

The homepage communicates:

- The mission of Karo Pitch  
- How the pitching process works  
- Startup categories that can participate  
- Investors looking for promising startups  
- Example featured startups  
- A strong call-to-action to apply

# Why This Design
The design approach was based on three core principles:

### 1. Clarity
Founders should quickly understand what Karo Pitch is and how it works.

### 2. Credibility
Investors and founders should feel that the platform is professional and trustworthy.

### 3. Conversion
The page strongly encourages founders to **apply to pitch their startup**.
